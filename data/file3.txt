Rafael Nadal is a professional tennis player from Spain. He is famous for his heavy topspin forehand, 
incredible athleticism, and fighting spirit. Nadal is called the "King of Clay" because he has won the 
French Open a record fourteen times. He is left-handed and known for his intense pre-serve rituals. 
Outside of tennis, Nadal loves playing golf and runs the Rafa Nadal Academy in Mallorca, Spain.
