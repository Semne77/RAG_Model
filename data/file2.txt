Novak Djokovic is a professional tennis player from Serbia. He is known for his exceptional flexibility, 
return of serve, and mental toughness on the court. Djokovic is the only male player to have won all nine 
ATP Masters 1000 tournaments. He is also recognized for his plant-based diet and meditation practice. 
Off the court, Djokovic speaks Serbian, English, Italian, and French. He founded the Novak Djokovic Foundation,
 which supports early childhood education in Serbia.
